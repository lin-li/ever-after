      Our Love story

      Sep, 2013

      First Met

      How can I forget the time when I first met you? I remember clearly that night at Peking University. I went there for a lecture about violin collection. You were wearing a long skirt, elegant and cute. I never thought that beautiful lady sitting far away from me would become my girlfriend 9 months later.

      Dec, 2013

      Playing Violin

      It is the happiest time in my life to play violin with you at Peking University. Life was so cozy and unrealistic at that time, just like the movie Roman Times. I could hardly image the days without you when I returned to Shanghai. I didn't know how to say goodbye. I couldn't think of any words. I would cherish my visit here in memory as long as I live.

      May, 2014

      In a Relationship

      Finally, you became my girlfriend! I was downstairs of your dormitory, with flowers in my hand. I was really nervous actually. You came downstairs and hugged me. I took you to Weiming Lake and told you that I love you. I was so happy when you said yes. We have been in love for more than one year. Thank you for keeping me grounded, and for helping me keep my sense of wonder about the world as we grow together.

      Aug, 2014

      Far or Near

      Foreign love was never easy for us. I was studying computer science at Los Angeles, and you were staying in Beijing and applying to study Education at US. There seems to be such a long physical distance between us. However, We all know that our hearts are near together because we are in love. Even though we are miles apart, you are never far from my heart.

      March, 2015

      San Diego

      You came to visit me during spring break. I rent a car and took you to San Diego. SD is a beautiful city, La Jolla, Subset Cliff, Coronado Beach... It was our first time to travel abroad. Life is so simple and easy. Just you, me and a car. I can take you wherever we want. One day, we will have our own car, and our own hourse. I hope the house can be near a beach. We can go for a walk to the beach after super every day. What a wonderful life!
